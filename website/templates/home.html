Duk container Pools are the only container pool made in the USA by American Welding Society (AWS) certified craftsman. Starting with a single use shipping container, we fabricate custom container pools and ship world wide, to your various destinations. It is our goal to provide you a strong and durable pool that will last for many years. After hand selecting each container, we work with you to design the perfect configuration to suit your location. Then we go to work fabricating the container, prepping and lining the bare sheet metal with industry approved products and finally outfitting your Duk pool with all the finishing touches.
